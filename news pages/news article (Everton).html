MOYES MASTERCLASS: TARKOWSKI AND DEWSBURY-HALL SINK BURNLEY

FULL TIME ● HILL DICKINSON STADIUM, LIVERPOOL

Everton delivered a professional and dominant performance to see off Burnley, with David Moyes overseeing a tactical "masterclass" that left the visitors with no answer. The Toffees controlled the tempo from the outset, ensuring a comfortable evening at the Hill Dickinson Stadium.

James Tarkowski set the tone against his former club, rising highest to fire Everton into the lead in the 32nd minute. The victory was effectively wrapped up on the hour mark when Kiernan Dewsbury-Hall found the net to make it 2-0, a lead the home side defended with total composure to secure a clean sheet.

The Manager's View

Reflecting on the clinical display, David Moyes commented:

"It was a professional performance from start to finish. We wanted to be aggressive early on, and James gave us that platform. Once we got the second goal, our game management was excellent.

Everton: 2 (Tarkowski 32', Dewsbury-Hall 60')
