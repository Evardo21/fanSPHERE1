CUP MAGIC: PORT VALE SECURE HISTORIC FA CUP FOURTH ROUND UPSET

FULL TIME ● VALE PARK, STOKE-ON-TRENT

Port Vale produced a performance that will go down in club folklore, securing a historic FA Cup Fourth Round upset against Bristol City. The Valiants showed immense discipline and grit to take the contest deep into extra time before finding a way to sink their higher-league opponents

The tension at Vale Park was palpable as the game remained scoreless through 90 minutes. However, the breakthrough finally arrived in the 112th minute when Ben Waine found the back of the net, sparking scenes of wild celebration and booking Vale's place in the next round.

The Manager's View

The manager hailed the historic result at the final whistle:

"This is what the FA Cup is all about. These players gave everything for 120 minutes, and to get that winning goal in front of our fans is a moment we will never forget. We've written a new chapter for this club today."

MATCH STATISTICS
Port Vale: 1 (Waine 112')
Bristol City: 0
