MOLINEUX MAYHEM: WOLVES STUN LIVERPOOL WITH LAST-GASP STRIKE AS REDS STUMBLE AGAIN

FULL TIME ● MOLINEUX STADIUM, WOLVERHAMPTON

Wolverhampton Wanderers secured a dramatic victory over Liverpool, dealing a significant blow to the Reds' campaign with a final-minute winner at Molineux. The hosts showcased incredible resilience to overcome a late scare and take all three points in front of a raucous home crowd.

The match ignited in the 78th minute when Rodrigo Gomes broke the deadlock to put Wolves ahead. Liverpool appeared to have rescued a point when Mohamed Salah leveled the scoring in the 83rd minute, but the drama was far from over. As the game entered stoppage time, Andre pounced in the 94th minute to seal a famous win for the Old Gold.

The Manager's View

Speaking to the press after the final whistle, the manager praised the squad's character:

"To win it in that fashion, especially after conceding so late, shows the incredible belief in this group. We stayed aggressive until the very last second, and that energy from the fans at Molineux carried us over the line."

MATCH STATISTICS
Wolverhampton: 2 (Gomes 78', Andre 94')
Liverpool: 1 (Salah 83')
